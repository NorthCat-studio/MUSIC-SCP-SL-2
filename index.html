News SCP SL

Hello everyone! We just released the Halloween Seasonal Update for SCP:SL.

While we're hard at work preparing for the Patreon Beta 'Heavy Duty' release, we thought it would be a good idea to dust off last year's Halloween Update. Introducing everyone's favorite candy SCP-330, SCP-3114, and other small changes.

Major Changes

  • SCP-330's special candy has returned, with some minor differences from 2023.
  • Candy drop table has been adjusted slightly.
  • Brown Candy can no longer be obtained from SCP-330. Its effect can still be obtained using Black Candy.
  • Green Candy has been improved. Its effect now lasts longer and increases maximum health to 300 points (previously 200).
  • Fixed an issue where Gray Candy did not apply the intended damage reduction.
  • Blue Candy no longer allows friendly fire. The effect for Evil Candy has been retained.
  • Evil Candy now allows you to open checkpoint doors after consumption.
  • SCP-3114 has been returned with a reduced chance of appearing for the duration of the event.
  • Special skins for SCPs, human classes, items, and more have been returned.
  • New environments, graphics, and sound effects have been added to feel the Halloween spirit.
